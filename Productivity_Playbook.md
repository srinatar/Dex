# Productivity Playbook — Daily & Weekly Systems

**One-page reference** for the systems and practices that make Dex 10x useful. Do the daily and weekly rituals; use the capture and review tools so nothing slips.

---

## Every day

| When | Do this | Why |
|------|---------|-----|
| **Morning** | `/daily-plan` | Aligns today with calendar, tasks, and week priorities. One place to see “what matters today.” |
| **Before any meeting** | `/meeting-prep` | Pulls context on attendees, related projects, and recent notes so you show up prepared. |
| **When someone asks for something** | `/backlog-capture` — “Add to backlog: [ask] from [Field\|Peer\|Support]” | Nothing from field, peers, or support gets lost. One inbox to triage later. |
| **After meetings** | Share notes or say “I had a meeting about X” | Dex can extract tasks, update person pages, and link to projects. |
| **When you finish a task** | “Mark [task] done” or “I finished X” | Task gets completed everywhere (Tasks.md, meeting notes, person/project pages). |
| **End of day** | `/daily-review` or `/review` | Close the loop: what got done, what to carry, learnings captured. |

**Daily rule:** One plan in the morning, one review at night. Capture asks into the backlog as they arrive.

---

## Every week

| When | Do this | Why |
|------|---------|-----|
| **Start of week (Mon or Sun)** | `/week-plan` | Set 3–5 weekly priorities from quarter goals and projects. Calendar shape + capacity inform what you can do. |
| **Mid or end of week** | `/project-health` | See which projects are on track, stale, or blocked. Fix before it’s too late. |
| **When backlog grows** | `/backlog-review` | Triage Field/Peer/Support items: promote to tasks or projects, or defer. Inbox stays actionable. |
| **End of week (Fri)** | `/week-review` | Synthesis of what shipped, what didn’t, goal progress, and suggested next-week priorities. |
| **After week-review** | `/email-weekly-synthesis` | Get the weekly synthesis in your inbox (or a file to paste into Gmail) for reading or sharing. |

**Weekly rule:** One plan at the start, one review at the end. Triage backlog and project health at least once.

## Projects: one place to edit

| What | Where | Then |
|------|--------|-----|
| **Edit all projects** | `04-Projects/ALL_PROJECTS_ONE_VIEW.md` | Add, change, or remove project sections in one doc. No need to open six files. |
| **Push to Dex** | `/sync-projects` | Writes the one-view back to individual project files so `/project-health` and other skills see the latest. |
| **Check status** | `/project-health` | Quick view of which projects need attention. |

**Rule:** All project edits in the one-view → sync when done. Review project health weekly.

## Capture (so nothing is lost)

| Incoming thing | Where it goes | How |
|----------------|---------------|-----|
| **Field / Peer / Support asks** | `00-Inbox/Backlog_Inbox.md` | `/backlog-capture` or add a row. Source = Field \| Peer \| Support. |
| **Meeting notes** | `00-Inbox/Meetings/` | Paste or describe; Dex can extract tasks and update people. |
| **Quick ideas** | `00-Inbox/Ideas/` | Drop a note. |
| **Tasks (from you)** | “Create a task to …” | Dex infers pillar; task lands in `03-Tasks/Tasks.md` and can link to projects. |

**Rule:** One funnel for “someone asked for something” → Backlog Inbox. One funnel for “I need to do something” → Tasks (or week priorities).

## Review & prioritize (so you choose what to do)

| Review | Command / place | Purpose |
|--------|------------------|--------|
| **Backlog** | `/backlog-review` | Turn pending asks into tasks, project items, or deferred. |
| **Inbox / orphans** | `/triage` | Route stray files and scattered tasks to projects or people. |
| **Week** | `/week-review` | Summarize week, goal progress, and next-week focus. |
| **Quarter** | `/quarter-review` | End-of-quarter: what shipped, what didn’t, learnings. |

**Rule:** Backlog and inbox get triaged on a schedule (e.g. weekly). Week and quarter get a formal review.

## 10x habits (systems that compound)

1. **Single source of truth**  
   Projects: edit only `ALL_PROJECTS_ONE_VIEW.md`, then `/sync-projects`. One place to look and update.

2. **Capture first, prioritize later**  
   Dump every field/peer/support ask into the backlog with `/backlog-capture`. Batch decisions in `/backlog-review` instead of deciding in the moment.

3. **Plan from the top**  
   Quarter goals → week priorities → daily plan. Run `/week-plan` and `/daily-plan` so today ties to the week and quarter.

4. **Prep every meeting**  
   `/meeting-prep` before calls. Fewer “I’ll get back to you” and more “here’s the answer.”

5. **Close the loop daily**  
   `/daily-review` or `/review` so done vs not-done is explicit and learnings are captured.

6. **One health check per week**  
   `/project-health` + `/backlog-review` so projects and incoming asks don’t drift.

7. **Email your week**  
   `/email-weekly-synthesis` after `/week-review` so you (and optionally others) get the narrative in inbox.

8. **Person pages as your memory**  
   Check `05-Areas/People/` before deep dives; keep person pages updated so context is always there.

9. **Pillars and projects on every task**  
   When creating or triaging tasks, tag project and pillar. That keeps work aligned and visible in project health.

10. **Batch similar work**  
    Triage once a week. Sync projects when you’ve finished editing the one-view. Plan the week once; adjust daily.
